Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about n8n

What is n8n and how does it work?

n8n is a powerful workflow automation platform that allows you to connect apps and automate complex business processes without coding. It works by creating "workflows" - sequences of actions triggered by specific events. You can connect hundreds of apps, set conditions, transform data, and execute actions automatically. The visual workflow builder makes it easy for anyone to create sophisticated automations by dragging and dropping nodes, and the platform handles the execution, error handling, and monitoring behind the scenes.

Do I need technical skills to use n8n?

No technical skills are required to get started with n8n. Our visual workflow builder is designed for non-technical users, allowing business analysts, marketers, and operations professionals to create automations without writing code. However, for advanced use cases, n8n also supports custom JavaScript code, making it powerful enough for developers and technical teams. The platform is truly flexible - simple for beginners, powerful for experts.

How many apps can I connect with n8n?

n8n supports over 400 native integrations with popular business applications including Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Google Workspace, Microsoft Teams, Stripe, GitHub, and many more. Additionally, you can connect virtually any application using webhooks, REST APIs, or custom HTTP requests. This means you're not limited to pre-built integrations - if an app has an API, you can integrate it with n8n. We're constantly adding new integrations based on user requests.

What kind of tasks can I automate with n8n?

You can automate virtually any repetitive business task. Common use cases include: lead qualification and assignment, customer data synchronization, invoice generation and payment processing, social media posting, email campaigns, report generation, ticket management, data backups, and much more. Whether you need simple one-step automations or complex multi-step workflows with conditional logic, n8n can handle it. The possibilities are limited only by your imagination and your business needs.

What is the pricing and how do I get started?

n8n offers flexible pricing options to suit businesses of all sizes. We have a free tier to get started, as well as paid plans with additional features, execution capacity, and support. You can self-host n8n for complete control, or use our cloud platform for convenience. To get started, simply sign up for a free account on our website - no credit card required. You'll have immediate access to our full platform and can start building workflows in minutes. Our team is ready to help with onboarding and answering any questions.

About n8n

n8n was founded in 2019 by Jan Oberhauser with a simple mission: to make workflow automation accessible to everyone. What started as a personal project to solve workflow automation challenges has grown into a global platform used by hundreds of thousands of users across enterprises, startups, and organizations of all sizes. The name "n8n" represents the infinite possibilities of automation - the "n" stands for any number, symbolizing unlimited potential. Today, n8n operates as an open-source project with a thriving community of developers, users, and contributors who believe in democratizing automation technology.

At n8n, we're committed to three core values: accessibility (making automation available to everyone, not just developers), flexibility (supporting any workflow and any integration), and transparency (being open about our roadmap and involving our community in our development). We believe that automation should empower teams, not replace them. By automating repetitive tasks, we free people to focus on creative, strategic, and high-value work. Whether you're a startup looking to scale efficiently or an enterprise seeking to optimize operations, n8n provides the tools and support you need to succeed in an increasingly automated world. Our mission is to make workflow automation the foundation of modern business operations.
